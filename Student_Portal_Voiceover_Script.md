# VALLEY VIEW UNIVERSITY SRC STUDENT PORTAL
## VOICE-OVER SCRIPT (12-Minute Version)

---

## INTRODUCTION [30 seconds]

Welcome to the Valley View University Students' Representative Council Portal. This comprehensive system connects over 1,500 students with their SRC leadership, providing seamless access to elections, events, news, documents, welfare services, and more. Let's explore how this platform empowers you as a student.

---

## PART 1: PUBLIC WEBSITE [2 minutes]

### Homepage (index.php)

The homepage features a dynamic hero slider showcasing three key themes: the SRC identity, student representation, and leadership development. 

Quick access buttons at the top connect you to the Student Portal, E-Learning platform, and iSchool system. 

Below, live statistics display active students, annual events, portfolios, and departments. The upcoming events section shows dates, times, and locations for activities like the Christmas Carol Night, Leadership Summit, Career Fair, and Sports Competition.

Latest news keeps you informed about election schedules, orientation success stories, and scholarship achievements.

### About Page (about.php)

The About page outlines our mission: representing student interests, providing welfare support, organizing events, fostering inclusivity, and facilitating communication with administration.

Our vision emphasizes student empowerment, diversity, innovation, strong partnerships, and developing well-rounded graduates.

Executive portfolios showcase SRC leadership with contact information and responsibilities.

### Events, News & Contact (events.php, news.php, contact.php)

The Events page displays upcoming activities with date badges, timing, locations, and registration details. 

The News section presents articles in a grid layout with categories, images, and pagination for easy browsing.

Contact Us provides three methods: phone (+233 54 881 1774), email (officialsrcvvu@gmail.com), and a contact form. Office hours and an interactive campus map help you locate the SRC office.

---

## PART 2: LOGIN & AUTHENTICATION [1 minute]

### Login Page (login.php)

The login page features a video background with a modern glassmorphism design.

Enter your student email and password. Toggle password visibility with the eye icon. Use "Remember Me" for convenience, or "Forgot Password" if needed.

The system includes security features: password encryption, account lockout after failed attempts, and IP monitoring. First-time users must change their default password before accessing the portal.

---

## PART 3: STUDENT DASHBOARD [1.5 minutes]

### Dashboard Overview (dashboard.php)

After login, your dashboard displays a gradient header welcoming you to the SRC management system.

Statistics cards show real-time counts for events, news, documents, gallery items, elections, and portfolios.

The Upcoming Events widget displays color-coded status badges—Today, Upcoming, or Ongoing—with dates, times, and locations.

Recent News shows the three latest articles with publication dates and authors.

The Activity Feed tracks system updates, user actions, and content changes with timestamps like "2 hours ago" or "1 day ago."

Based on your role, quick action buttons provide shortcuts to common tasks.

---

## PART 4: KEY STUDENT FEATURES [6 minutes]

### Elections (elections.php) [45 seconds]

The Elections page displays active campaigns with titles, descriptions, voting periods, and status indicators.

To vote: view all candidates, read manifestos, select your choice, and confirm. Your vote is encrypted and anonymous with real-time confirmation.

Concluded elections show visual charts with vote distribution, percentages, winner announcements, and turnout statistics.

### Documents (documents.php) [30 seconds]

Access the SRC Constitution, guidelines, policies, meeting minutes, reports, and forms.

Each document shows its name, file type, size, and upload date. Search by name, filter by category, or sort by date. Download or preview documents directly from the portal.

### Feedback System (feedback.php) [45 seconds]

Submit feedback in categories: Academic Issues, Welfare Concerns, Event Suggestions, General Feedback, or Complaints.

Enter a subject and detailed message. Choose to submit anonymously or with your name. Attach supporting documents if needed.

After submission, receive a reference number to track your feedback. The SRC team reviews within 48 hours. Non-anonymous submissions receive responses, and you can add follow-up comments.

### Welfare Services (welfare.php) [45 seconds]

The Welfare page shows statistics: total requests, pending cases, resolved issues, and average response time.

Submit requests for Financial Assistance, Medical Support, Accommodation Issues, Mental Health Support, or Emergency Assistance.

Provide your details, select a category, describe your situation, specify urgency, and upload supporting documents like medical reports or bills.

Track your request status—Pending, Under Review, Approved, or Denied—view assigned officers, and communicate with the welfare team.

### Events Management (events.php - student view) [30 seconds]

Browse upcoming events, filter by type or date, and view complete details including organizers and registration requirements.

For events requiring registration, click "Register," fill in your information, receive confirmation, and add the event to your calendar with reminder notifications.

### Gallery (gallery.php) [30 seconds]

The Gallery displays photos in a responsive grid with event names, dates, and photo counts.

Click any album to view photos in full-screen mode with zoom, navigation, slideshow options, and download capabilities. Share photos on social media or view details like date, event, and photographer.

### Committees & Departments [45 seconds]

Explore SRC committees including Executive, Electoral Commission, Finance, Welfare, Events, Academic, and Sports committees.

Each committee page shows its purpose, current members, meeting schedules, activities, and how to join.

The Departments section displays all SRC departments with heads, descriptions, and contact information. Click any department to see its mission, members, projects, and resources.

### Senate & Governance (senate.php) [30 seconds]

Learn about senate composition, roles, and responsibilities. View current members with their faculties, positions, and contact information.

Access public resources including senate resolutions, meeting minutes, the senate constitution, and understand how decisions impact students.

### Reports & Minutes [30 seconds]

Access Financial Reports with income, expenditure, and budgets; Activity Reports showing events and impacts; Project Reports detailing implementation status; and Term/Annual Reports.

Meeting Minutes are searchable by date range, meeting type, or keyword, showing attendees, agenda items, decisions, action points, and responsible persons with deadlines.

### Profile & Settings (profile.php, change-password.php) [30 seconds]

Manage your personal information: name, student ID, email, phone, program, level, and department.

Upload or change your profile photo with cropping tools. Set email notification preferences, privacy settings, and language options.

Change your password regularly using the secure form with strength indicators. Passwords require 8+ characters with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.

### Support Resources (support pages) [30 seconds]

Access FAQs organized by topics: Account Issues, Portal Usage, Elections, Events, Welfare, and Documents.

Watch video tutorials on login, navigation, feedback submission, voting, and document downloads.

Use live chat during office hours for real-time support, or submit a ticket for technical issues with priority levels, screenshots, and status tracking.

### Key System Advantages

This integrated portal provides:
- **Transparency**: Real-time access to SRC activities, finances, and decisions
- **Engagement**: Easy participation in elections, events, and feedback
- **Communication**: Direct channels to SRC officers and welfare services
- **Information**: Centralized access to documents, news, and resources
- **Accountability**: Track requests, view reports, and monitor SRC performance

The Valley View University SRC Portal is designed to enhance your student experience through seamless digital access to all SRC services.

Whether voting in elections, accessing documents, submitting feedback, seeking welfare support, or staying updated with news and events, this platform connects you with your student leadership.

The SRC is here to serve you and represent your interests. Log in today to explore all available features.
